About Cricket Batting Analyzer

Our Mission

We're revolutionizing cricket training by bringing professional-level shot analysis to players of all levels. Our mission is to make advanced batting analysis accessible, affordable, and easy to understand.

Whether you're a weekend cricketer or an aspiring professional, our tools help you understand your technique and identify areas for improvement.

The Technology

Using cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning algorithms, our system analyzes your batting videos to identify key technical elements:

  • Shot type classification (cover drive, pull shot, etc.)
  • Bat swing path analysis
  • Footwork and balance assessment
  • Timing and contact point evaluation
